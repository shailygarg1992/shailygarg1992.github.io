About

Builder. Founder.
Product Leader.

I'm a product leader who has spent a decade doing one thing: building systems that scale. From launching AI-powered marketplaces to leading agentic delivery models at Walmart, I work at the intersection of product intuition, applied ML, and operational excellence.

At Walmart, I lead Last Mile Pricing & Customer Promise as Lead PM — building agentic AI systems with 30+ engineers and data scientists that serve 6.5M+ customers across the US, Canada, Chile, and Mexico, delivering $45M in annual savings while maintaining 97% on-time delivery.

Before Walmart, I founded GlobalFair — a cross-border B2B marketplace for building materials. We built a three-sided marketplace (buyers ↔ suppliers ↔ logistics), scaled to $50M ARR, achieved 300% YoY growth and 93% customer retention, and were acquired in 2024.

I'm an IIT Bombay alumna and a Forbes 30 Under 30 honoree (2022) for AI-driven startup innovation. Currently building two AI-native side projects on nights and weekends.
